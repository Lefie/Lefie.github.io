PPLKPR

What is pplkpr?

it is both a real app and an art project.

pplkpr is an ios app that you can install on your phone.

It also requires the use of a smartwatch to monitor your emotional / physical response to people around you


What is the goal pplkpr as an ios app?

the goal of the app is to collect data based on your response to the people you interact with. It then analyzes the data and makes decisions on who you should and should not be hanging out with .


What is the goal pplkpr as an art project?

the art project is meant to examine the implications of quantified living for relationships


What are technologies that are involved ?

GPS and a heart rate wristband. GPS to track if where you are coming and going. Heart rate wristband tracks if you have a change of emotional state


How it works
Step1 : You have put on the wristband and installed software
Step2: PPLKPR sends you a notification when it detects a change of location or heart rate
Step3 : You fill out a report including info such as who you were with, how they made you feel etc and PPLKPR analyzes the data
Step4 : pplkpr gives you a breakdown of who's affecting you positively and negatively
Step5 : acts on your behalf to invite people to hang out / send messages / block / delete contacts
