Solmaz Sharif

Solmaz Sharif is an Iranian-American poet, writer, and former United States Navy servicemember. She was born in Istanbul, Turkey in 1983, and later immigrated to the United States with her family. Sharif earned her MFA in poetry from the University of California, Berkeley and has since published several highly-acclaimed collections of poetry, including "Look" which was a finalist for the National Book Award in 2016.

In her work, Sharif explores themes related to war, violence, and the power of language. She often draws on her own experiences as a former Navy veteran, as well as her family's history as Iranian immigrants to the United States.

Sharif's writing has been featured in numerous publications and anthologies, including The New York Times, Poetry magazine, and The Best American Poetry. She has received many awards and honors for her work, including the Rona Jaffe Foundation Writers' Award and a fellowship from the National Endowment for the Arts.
