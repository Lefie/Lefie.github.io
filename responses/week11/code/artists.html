PPLKPR
  • An LA based computer media artist who examinies social relationships in the midst of surveillance, automation, and algorithmic living
  • The creator of p5.js, an open-source art and education platform that prioritizes access and diversity in learning to code with over 10 million users.
  • Lauren is currently a Professor at UCLA Design Media Arts. She holds an MFA from UCLA and a BS Computer Science and BS Art and Design from MIT.
  • An artist who works with code, he crafts interactive installations, playful websites, workshops, and toolkits for other artists who also work with code.
  • His works explore possibilities of new technologies: to understand how they affect society, to misuse them, and build alternative futures
  • He works with technologies such as machine learning, computer vision, social and surveillance tech spanning commercial and arts spaces
